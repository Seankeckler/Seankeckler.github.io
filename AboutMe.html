While performing these jobs I was asked to transition over to the used car department. When I was at a loss, I would turn to the internet, as most people do. I would find myself, more often than not, finding the answers quickly but without the actual physical knowledge of how to perform the tasks, still taking longer than I would have liked on the jobs.
